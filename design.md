# CaliCore Academy — Website Design Brief

## 1. Brand Essence
CaliCore is a calisthenics, gymnastics, and strength academy in Laxmi Nagar, Delhi. The brand identity centers on fire, discipline, and bodyweight mastery — a "forge" where strength is built, not bought. Visual language should feel gritty, intense, industrial, and motivational — closer to a dojo/training ground than a commercial gym chain.

Core tagline: "Where Strength Is Built. Where Discipline Is Forged. Where Champions Rise."

---

## 2. Color Palette

Primary — Fire & Forge (from logo)
- Charcoal Black: #0D0D0D (backgrounds, base)
- Deep Slate: #1A1A1D (section backgrounds, cards)
- Ember Orange: #FF4D1C (primary accent — CTAs, highlights)
- Molten Gold: #F4A623 (secondary accent — headings, icons, badges)
- Flame Red: #B8291E (gradient support, hover states)

Neutral / Text
- Off-White: #F5F2EC (primary text on dark)
- Steel Grey: #8C8C8C (secondary text, captions)
- Concrete Grey: #2E2E30 (borders, dividers)

Usage guidance
- Background stays dark (black/charcoal) throughout — mirrors the gym's actual interior and IG aesthetic.
- Gold/orange gradient (logo flame) used sparingly for headings, icons, buttons, and section dividers — not flooded across the whole page.
- Avoid bright whites; use warm off-white for readability against dark backgrounds.

---

## 3. Typography

Headings: A bold, condensed, slightly aggressive display font — e.g. "Anton", "Bebas Neue", or "Oswald" (all free Google Fonts). Conveys strength, intensity, and the "academy crest" feel.

Body: A clean, highly readable sans-serif — e.g. "Inter", "Work Sans", or "Manrope". Keeps long-form text (about, programs, testimonials) easy to scan.

Accent/Numerals (stats, pricing, badges): Same as heading font, used in caps with letter-spacing for a "stamped" look.

Hierarchy
- H1: Bebas Neue / Anton, 48–72px, uppercase, tight letter spacing
- H2/H3: Same family, 28–40px, uppercase
- Body: Inter, 16–18px, regular/medium weight
- Buttons/CTAs: Oswald or Bebas Neue, uppercase, letter-spaced

---

## 4. Visual & UI Style Notes
- Dark, moody, high-contrast — fire/flame textures or gradients as background accents (hero, section dividers).
- Real gym photos/reels as hero backgrounds with dark overlay for text legibility.
- Logo shield (flame "C") used as a recurring motif — favicon, section markers, badges.
- Rounded corners kept minimal — sharp edges, angular dividers feel more "forged steel."
- Subtle motion: hover glow on buttons (ember-orange glow), scroll-fade-ins for sections.
- Badges for "5.0 ★ Google Rating", "1st 50 Members Offer", etc. styled like gym medals/stamps.

---

## 5. Site Structure / Pages

### 1. Home
- Hero: Full-bleed gym photo/video background, logo, tagline, primary CTA ("Book a Free Trial" / "Join Now")
- Quick intro (1–2 lines from About)
- Programs preview (grid of 6–7 cards: Calisthenics, Gymnastics, MMA/Boxing, Yoga, Kids Power, Female Batch, Endurance)
- Why CaliCore (key differentiators, 5.0 rating badge)
- Testimonials carousel/strip
- Location + CTA strip (address, map, WhatsApp button)
- Footer

### 2. About Us
- Brand story (origin, philosophy — "Cali" + "Core", fire-forged identity)
- Mission/values (skill-based, equipment-light, community-driven)
- Facility highlights (industrial space, mural, rigs, bars)
- Coaches/trainers section (if photos/bios available later)

### 3. Programs / Classes
- Detailed cards/sections for each: Calisthenics & Bodyweight, Gymnastics (Kids & Advanced), MMA & Boxing, Yoga, Functional Strength, Core Strength, Female Batch, Kids Power, Endurance, Self-Defense
- Each with short description, who it's for, and a relevant photo

### 4. Gallery / Community
- Photo/video grid pulling from IG content (training shots, mural, workshops, kid competitions)
- Optional embedded reels

### 5. Testimonials / Reviews
- Google reviews showcased with star ratings
- Optional "success stories" / transformation highlights

### 6. Pricing / Offers
- Membership tiers (if available)
- Launch offer banner (1st 50 members – 15% off)

### 7. Contact / Location
- Address, map embed, phone/WhatsApp button, Instagram link
- Hours of operation
- Simple contact/enquiry form

### 8. Footer (global)
- Logo + tagline
- Quick links (Programs, About, Contact)
- Social icons (Instagram @calicore1)
- Address + phone
- Copyright

---

## 6. Tone of Voice
Bold, motivational, no-nonsense — short punchy sentences, action verbs ("Train. Build. Rise."), occasional fire/forge metaphors, inclusive of all ages and skill levels (beginners welcome, kids to advanced athletes).
